📱 Naal Kaati – Support & User Guide

📱 About the App

Naal Kaati is a comprehensive Indian calendar app that provides accurate Panchangam details across multiple regional calendars. It helps users stay connected with traditional timekeeping, auspicious days, and astrology insights.

🌍 Supported Calendars & Languages

📅 Calendars Supported

  • Tamil Calendar
  • Telugu Calendar
  • Kannada Calendar

🌐 Languages Supported

  • English
  • Tamil
  • Telugu
  • Kannada

👉 You can switch between calendars and languages based on your preference.

🪔 Tamil Calendar Panchangam Types

  • Thirukanida Panchangam – Based on astronomical calculations
  • Vakya Panchangam – Based on traditional/ancient calculation methods

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Open the Naal Kaati app
  2. Select Calendar & Language
  3. Select Panchangam type (Tamil only)
  4. Allow location access for accurate timings

📅 Daily Panchangam Details

  • Date (based on selected calendar)
  • Day (Kizhamai / Vara)
  • Tithi
  • Nakshatram
  • Yogam & Karanam
  • Sunrise & Sunset
  • Moonrise & Moonset

🪔 Auspicious & Inauspicious Timings

  • Rahu Kalam – Avoid important activities
  • Yamagandam – Inauspicious period
  • Kuligai – Neutral timing

🪷 Muhurta & Important Days

  • Muhurta Naal
  • Festival days
  • Amavasai & Pournami
  • Other important days

🤖 AI Astrology Chat

Ask astrology-related questions using Gemini AI. You can ask about auspicious timings, Panchangam details, and general astrology topics.

⚙️ Settings

  • Change calendar type
  • Switch language
  • Select Panchangam type
  • Manage preferences

🔒 Privacy & Location

The app uses location only for accurate sunrise, sunset, and Panchangam calculations.

❓ FAQ

Why location access?
To calculate accurate timings based on your region.

Thirukanida vs Vakya?
Astronomical vs traditional calculation methods.

Can I switch calendars?
Yes, anytime.
