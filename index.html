# Naal Kaati – Support & User Guide ## 📱 About the App Naal Kaati is a comprehensive Indian calendar app that provides accurate Panchangam details across multiple regional calendars. It helps users stay connected with traditional timekeeping, auspicious days, and astrology insights. --- ## 🌍 Supported Calendars & Languages ### 📅 Calendars Supported * Tamil Calendar * Telugu Calendar * Kannada Calendar ### 🌐 Languages Supported * English * Tamil * Telugu * Kannada 👉 You can switch between calendars and languages based on your preference. --- ## 🪔 Tamil Calendar Panchangam Types Naal Kaati supports two traditional Tamil Panchangam calculation methods: * **Thirukanida Panchangam** – Based on astronomical calculations * **Vakya Panchangam** – Based on traditional/ancient calculation methods 👉 Users can choose their preferred Panchangam type for Tamil calendar calculations. --- ## 🚀 Getting Started ### 1. Open the App Launch the Naal Kaati app from your device. ### 2. Select Calendar & Language * Choose your preferred calendar (Tamil / Telugu / Kannada) * Select your preferred language ### 3. Select Panchangam Type (Tamil Only) * Choose **Thirukanida** or **Vakya** Panchangam ### 4. Allow Location Access * The app uses your location to calculate: * Sunrise & Sunset * Moonrise & Moonset * Accurate Panchangam timings 👉 This ensures highly accurate daily calculations. --- ## 📅 Daily Panchangam Details The home screen displays: * Date (based on selected calendar) * Day (Kizhamai / Vara) * Tithi (Lunar day) * Nakshatram (Star) * Yogam & Karanam * Sunrise & Sunset * Moonrise & Moonset --- ## 🪔 Auspicious & Inauspicious Timings * **Rahu Kalam** – Avoid important activities * **Yamagandam** – Inauspicious period * **Kuligai** – Neutral/conditional timing --- ## 🪷 Muhurta & Auspicious Days Naal Kaati highlights: * **Muhurta Naal (Auspicious days)** * Festival days * Amavasai (New Moon) * Pournami (Full Moon) * Other important traditional days --- ## 📆 Navigating Dates * Use **Next / Previous** buttons * View past and future dates * Explore Panchangam for any selected day --- ## 🤖 AI Astrology Chat Naal Kaati includes an AI-powered chat feature powered by Gemini AI. ### What you can do: * Ask astrology-related questions * Get guidance on: * Auspicious timings * General astrology queries * Panchangam-related information 👉 AI responses are for informational purposes only. --- ## ⚙️ Settings * Change calendar type * Switch language * Select Panchangam type (Tamil) * Manage notification preferences --- ## 🔒 Location Usage Naal Kaati uses your device location to: * Accurately calculate solar and lunar timings * Provide precise Panchangam details 👉 Location data is used only for calculation purposes. --- ## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) ### Q: Why does the app need location access?
